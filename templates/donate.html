Help Fund Us

Citizen Budget is sponsored by the BYU Open Source Lab. We rely on generous gifts from community members to support our work. Donations go directly to fund student internships in our lab, as we mentor their work on one of our projects. Contributions made directly to the university are tax deductible.

If you are interested in donating, please visit the BYU Giving site. Be sure to specify Computer Science Scholarships and Mentorships in the selection area and specify BYU Open Source Lab in the comments area.

You may also contact Dr. Daniel Zappala, the director of the lab, directly at daniel.zappala@gmail.com.
